Overview

This website summarises the AI learning journey described in the report. It's designed for beginners and those with limited time, offering after‑hours study slots (e.g., evenings and weekends) so you can progress at your own pace. Each week includes challenges and hidden gems to keep the journey engaging.

Extended Timeline (After Hours)

Week Focus After‑hours Study Plan Highlights
0 Assessment & On‑boarding Mon–Tue, 7–9 pm: Take diagnostic quiz and join the community.
Wed–Fri, 7–8 pm: Set up your learning journal.		 Determine your starting point and prepare your workspace.
1 Chat‑based Exploration Mon–Wed, 7–8 pm: Chat with AI to learn basic concepts.
Thu–Sun, 8–9 pm: Review the AI for Beginners curriculum.		 Discover AI fundamentals through conversation.
2 Vibe‑Coding Mon–Fri, 7–9 pm: Build simple apps using ChatGPT/Claude, Lovable, v0 and Zapier Agents.
Sat–Sun, 2–4 pm: Recreate the Plywood Cutting Visualizer and share your own app.		 Experience natural‑language programming and create your first micro‑app.
3 Programming & Math Mon–Thu, 7–9 pm: Complete Khan Academy math lessons.
Fri–Sun, 3–5 pm: Work through Python and data courses.		 Build the foundations needed for machine learning.
4 Classical ML Mon–Thu, 7–9 pm: Follow Andrew Ng’s ML course.
Fri–Sun, 3–5 pm: Do Google’s ML Crash Course and start a mini‑project.		 Learn regression, classification and evaluation.
5 Deep Learning Mon–Fri, 7–9 pm: Study neural networks via Deeplearning.ai or fast.ai.
Sat–Sun, 4–6 pm: Read free deep learning books and train a CNN.		 Explore modern AI techniques.
6 Generative Models & Prompt Engineering Mon–Thu, 7–8 pm: Learn about diffusion models and LLMs.
Fri–Sun, 8–9 pm: Practise crafting creative prompts.		 Create text and image outputs through smart prompting.
7 AI Agents & Frameworks Mon–Thu, 7–9 pm: Study agentic AI and frameworks (LangChain, CrewAI, etc.).
Sat–Sun, 3–5 pm: Build a simple agent and try Zapier Agents.		 Develop autonomous agents that can reason and act.
8 Capstone Project All week, 7–9 pm: Work on your final project and share it with the community. Build and deploy an end‑to‑end AI application.

Key Resources

  • AI for Beginners curriculum (Microsoft Azure) – free 12‑week/24‑lesson course covering classical and modern AI topics.
  • Beginner vibe‑coding tools – ChatGPT, Claude, Lovable, v0 and Zapier Agents for natural‑language programming.
  • Math & Programming courses – Khan Academy (Linear Algebra, Statistics), IBM’s Machine Learning with Python, Kaggle micro‑courses.
  • Machine Learning courses – Andrew Ng’s ML course, Google’s ML Crash Course, Elements of AI.
  • Deep Learning & Generative AI – Deeplearning.ai, fast.ai, free books like Deep Learning and Dive into Deep Learning.
  • Agentic AI frameworks – LangChain, CrewAI, AutoGen, Semantic Kernel.
